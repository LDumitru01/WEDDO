Practical Information

What is the dress code?

We request Black Tie Optional. Tuxedos and formal gowns are encouraged, but suits and cocktail dresses are also welcome. Please note the ceremony will be on grass.

Are children invited?

While we love your little ones, our wedding will be an adults-only event so that everyone can relax and enjoy the evening.

Transportation & Parking

Shuttles will be provided from the Grand Hotel Tremezzo. There is limited parking at the venue, so we highly recommend using the provided transport.
